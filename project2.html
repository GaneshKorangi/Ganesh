Bhuvan Solutions is an advance CAD/CAM center and offers world class laboratory testing services for in-house testing and quality assurance. By offering defect free products consistently, Bhuvan Solutions has created a niche for itself in the market and has captured both mindshare and market share in the domains it operates.

Incepted in the year 2015 with a vision to alter the design, manufacturing, metallurgy and tool design landscape, Bhuvan Solutions was conceptualized as a leading CAD/CAM/CAE/Tool design center to translate innovative ideas into reality. Right from the start we have ensured that we maintain sound and healthy relationships with all stakeholders is it students, professionals or business owners.

Ensuring record time to market has been our forte as it has been our experience that 30% of the profit is lost if the product is released in the market with a gap of six months. To reduce this time-lag we have embraced automation as a tool for competitive advantage to unlock more business opportunities and achieve growth in areas like product design, development and reverse engineering.

There is a total number of more than 30 people in this skilled team, After years of development, we gradually bloomed into a diversified manufacturing factory, with Product Design, Rapid Prototype, Molds Making, Plastic Injection Production, Die Casting Production, Precision CNC Machining part, Metal Stamping, Sheet Metal, Silicone and Painting, Powder Coating. Based with those senior professional technician, well-equipped machines, perfect quality control and assurance system.
